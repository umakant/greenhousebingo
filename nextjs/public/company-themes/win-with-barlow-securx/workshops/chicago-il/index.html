Chicago Diagnostic Launch Workshop

A hands-on day in Chicago to build, price, and launch your diagnostic testing business with confidence.

Jumpstart Your Diagnostic Testing Career

Launching a diagnostic testing business doesn't have to be overwhelming. Our training programs are designed to equip aspiring and established lab service providers with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to succeed in the testing industry.

Whether you're interested in DNA testing, drug testing, fingerprinting, background screening, or specimen collection services, our hands-on training delivers practical instruction backed by real-world industry experience.

Ready to Learn?

Every training program is designed to develop both technical expertise and business readiness. Participants gain a thorough understanding of industry procedures, documentation requirements, compliance standards, and operational workflows essential for delivering diagnostic testing services professionally and responsibly.

Our goal is to ensure you leave training with the knowledge, confidence, and practical skills needed to successfully operate testing services in real-world environments.

We offer a variety of training formats to fit your schedule and learning style. From hands-on, in-person classes and business mastermind sessions to virtual certification courses, each program provides expert instruction, interactive learning, and actionable strategies you can immediately apply to your business.
