You're Invited to Plant Bingo at Four Corners!

Join us for a fun-filled evening at Plant Bingo, where great company, complimentary adult beverages, light refreshments, and exciting rounds of bingo come together for a one-of-a-kind experience. Whether you're a passionate plant enthusiast, a longtime bingo fan, or simply looking for a unique night out with friends, Plant Bingo offers the perfect mix of laughter, relaxation, and community. Best of all, every attendee is guaranteed to go home with a plant, making everyone a winner.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get in?

After purchase you'll receive an email with a unique QR code for each ticket. Show the QR code at the door and we'll scan you in.

How many plants do I take home?

One plant per ticket purchased. Buy 2 tickets, take home 2 plants.

Can I buy tickets at the door?

No. Tickets are online only so we can guarantee a plant for every guest.

What about extra Bingo cards?

Your ticket includes 10 cards. You can add more for $5 each — during purchase or at the door if any are left.

Is there a fee?

A 3.5% card processing fee is added at checkout. Everything else you see is the final price.

What if I can't make it?

Tickets are transferable — forward your QR email to a friend. We do not offer refunds under 48 hours before the event.
